Websites I’ve Built

Christ Tabernacle Website

This project showcases a website I developed for Christ Tabernacle. The site allows visitors to learn more about the church, view upcoming events, access resources, and connect with the ministry. It includes a structured layout for sermons, leadership profiles, and future integration of livestream services.

The website is currently under construction — many more features like event registration, giving portal, and content uploads will be added. It is not live yet, but serves as a solid foundation for a professional church website.

Thabiso Mdlalose Website

This is a personal website built for Thabiso Mdlalose — a vibrant MC, entrepreneur, and artist. The website will be used to showcase his services, gallery, biography, and booking information.

Just like the previous project, this site is also under construction. Additional features such as social proof, client testimonials, booking calendar, and downloadable media kits are still being developed.

Gladness House of God Ministries Website

This project was created for Gladness House of God Ministries — a dynamic and growing church committed to spreading the Word of God and serving the community. The website includes key information about the church, upcoming events, and ways to connect with the ministry. It's mobile-responsive, fast-loading, and aligned with the church's black-and-white theme.

Whether you're a church, ministry, or faith-based organization, a professionally designed website is essential in today's digital age. This site serves as a great example of how your message can reach further with the right design and structure.
